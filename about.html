WHY US?

Since 2021, ShinyTomato has been dedicated to delivering premium car detailing services through our flagship offerings, Eco-Groom and Eco-Posh. We are committed to continuous improvement and the pursuit of excellence, ensuring that every customer receives an unparalleled detailing experience.

At ShinyTomato, sustainability is at the core of our mission. We use 99% less water in our car wash and cleaning services compared to traditional methods. Additionally, our detailing products contain negligible amounts of VOCs, significantly reducing our environmental impact. By choosing ShinyTomato, you are not only opting for luxury but also supporting a sustainable future.
